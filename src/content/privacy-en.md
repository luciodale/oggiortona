# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 26 April 2026**

## Data controller

Oggi a Ortona is run as a personal project by Lucio D'Alessandro. For any privacy request, write to **privacy@oggiaortona.com**.

## Data we collect

We only collect data needed to run the app.

**On sign-in (via Google or email):**

- Email address
- First and last name (if provided by the provider)
- Profile picture (if provided by the provider)
- User identifier generated by Clerk

**When you contribute content:**

- The information you publish about venues, events and stores (name, description, address, hours, photos, etc.)
- The link between you and the content you publish

**When you enable push notifications:**

- Browser endpoint and cryptographic keys provided by your browser to deliver notifications

**Automatically generated:**

- IP address (handled by Cloudflare for protection and routing, not persistently stored)
- Technical cookies (see dedicated section below)

We do not use analytics, advertising trackers or profiling tools.

## Legal basis

We process your data based on:

- **Performance of a contract** (art. 6.1.b GDPR) to give you access to the app and manage your content
- **Consent** (art. 6.1.a GDPR) for sending push notifications, revocable at any time from profile settings
- **Legitimate interest** (art. 6.1.f GDPR) for infrastructure security

## How we use the data

- Allow you to sign in and use the app
- Display your content to other users
- Send push notifications if you opt in
- Communicate important account updates

We do not sell or share your data for commercial or advertising purposes.

## Third-party services (data processors)

To run the app we use the following providers, all with adequate data protection safeguards:

- **Clerk** (USA, EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified) — authentication and user management
- **Cloudflare** (EU/Global) — hosting, CDN, database (D1) and push notification delivery
- **Google** (only if you choose "Sign in with Google") — provides name, email and picture at sign-in

## Data retention

- **User profile**: kept while the account is active. Deleted immediately when you delete your account from the profile page.
- **Published venues and stores**: permanently deleted on account deletion, because they need an active owner to keep hours, contacts and information up to date.
- **Future events**: deleted on account deletion (no longer maintainable).
- **Past events held at your venues or stores**: deleted along with the venue or store.
- **Other past events**: kept as historical record, but **anonymised** (link to your profile is removed).
- **Push subscriptions**: deleted immediately on unsubscribe or account deletion.
- **Cloudflare technical logs**: handled per Cloudflare's policy (typically 7 days).

## Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** — know what data we hold about you. Download it directly from your profile.
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data from profile settings or via email.
- **Erasure** — delete your account directly from your profile.
- **Portability** — download your data in JSON format from your profile.
- **Object** — object to processing for legitimate reasons.
- **Withdraw consent** — disable push notifications at any time from your profile.
- **Complaint** — file a complaint with the **Italian Data Protection Authority** (garanteprivacy.it) or your local supervisory authority.

## Cookies and local storage

We only use technically necessary cookies and storage, exempt from prior consent:

- `locale` — remembers your selected language (1 year)
- Clerk session cookies — required for authentication
- `localStorage` — light/dark theme preference, dismissed reminders
- `sessionStorage` — PWA install reminder dismissal

We do not use profiling, analytics or advertising cookies, so no consent banner is required.

## International transfers

Clerk is based in the USA and certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, which guarantees an adequate level of protection. Cloudflare operates globally distributed servers with EU-preferred configuration.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top reflects the most recent revision. For substantial changes we will notify you through the app.

## Contact

To exercise your rights or for any question: **privacy@oggiaortona.com**
