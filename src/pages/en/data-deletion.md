# Data Deletion

**Oggi a Ortona** respects your right to have your personal data deleted.

## How to request deletion

Send an email to **privacy@oggiaortona.com** with the subject "Data deletion" and include the email address associated with your account.

## What gets deleted

- Your user profile (name, email, profile picture)
- Your push notification subscriptions
- Any data associated with your account

## Timeline

Your request will be processed within **30 days**.

## Contact

For any questions: **privacy@oggiaortona.com**
