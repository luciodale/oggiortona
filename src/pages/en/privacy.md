# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2 April 2026**

## Data Controller

Oggi a Ortona — oggiaortona.com

## Data Collected

When you sign in via Google, Apple, or Facebook, we collect:

- Full name
- Email address
- Profile picture

This data is used solely to identify you within the application.

## Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is used to:

- Enable login and authentication
- Personalise your user experience
- Send push notifications (only if you opt in)

## Data Retention

Your data is kept as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion at any time.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except for the technical services required to operate the app:

- **Clerk** — authentication
- **Cloudflare** — hosting and delivery

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

## Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at: **privacy@oggiaortona.com**

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions: **privacy@oggiaortona.com**
